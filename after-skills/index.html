Breakfast

Banana Pancakes

Soft stacks with a gentle sweetness and a sunny finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mash bananas and whisk with eggs.
  2. Fold in flour and baking powder.
  3. Cook until golden and fluffy.
Lunch

Garden Veggie Wraps

Crunch, herbs, and creamy hummus in a tidy lunch roll.

Ingredients

  • 4 tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • Spinach leaves

Steps

  1. Spread hummus over each tortilla.
  2. Pack in sliced vegetables and greens.
  3. Roll tightly and slice in half.
Dinner

Chicken Stir-Fry

Quick and glossy with crisp vegetables and a savory glaze.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken breast
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 broccoli head
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in a hot pan.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry until bright.
  3. Season with soy sauce and serve warm.
Soup

Tomato Basil Soup

Rich, silky, and made to feel like a small ritual.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 cup broth

Steps

  1. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes, basil, and broth.
  3. Simmer and blend until smooth.
Seafood

Baked Salmon

Simple salmon with lemon, herbs, and a tender finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dill or parsley

Steps

  1. Season salmon with oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Top with lemon and herbs.
  3. Bake until flaky and tender.
Snack

Chocolate Oat Cookies

Soft-baked cookies with deep cocoa and a mellow crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a dough.
  2. Scoop onto a baking tray.
  3. Bake until set and lightly crisp.
Dinner

Beef Tacos

Loaded tacos with spiced meat and bright toppings.

Ingredients

  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 taco shells
  • Lettuce, salsa, cheese

Steps

  1. Brown beef with spices.
  2. Fill taco shells with the meat.
  3. Top with lettuce, salsa, and cheese.
Dinner

Vegetable Fried Rice

Fast, savory, and bright with egg and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs in a pan.
  2. Add vegetables and rice.
  3. Season with soy sauce and stir until hot.
Dessert

Berry Yogurt Parfait

A layered dessert with fruit, yogurt, and crunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Honey to drizzle

Steps

  1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in glasses.
  2. Repeat until filled.
  3. Finish with honey and serve cold.
Breakfast

Avocado Toast

Simple toast with creaminess and a fresh finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  3. Spread onto toast and top with tomatoes.
