Spaghetti Carbonara

⏱ 25 min 👥 2 servings Medium

A Roman classic that demands no cream — only eggs, Pecorino, guanciale, and the starchy kiss of pasta water. Mastering the emulsion is everything.

Ingredients
  • 350g spaghetti or rigatoni
  • 150g guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 3 large egg yolks + 1 whole egg
  • 80g Pecorino Romano, finely grated
  • Freshly cracked black pepper, abundant
  • Salt for pasta water
Method
  1. Cook pasta in well-salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. Render guanciale in a cold pan over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk eggs, Pecorino, and abundant black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Toss hot drained pasta into the guanciale pan off the heat.
  5. Add egg mixture and a splash of pasta water, tossing vigorously to emulsify into a glossy sauce. Serve immediately.

Butter Chicken

⏱ 55 min 👥 4 servings Medium

Charred yogurt-marinated chicken in a silky tomato-cream sauce fragrant with garam masala, ginger, and fenugreek.

Ingredients
  • 700g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt, 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream, 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves, 1-inch ginger
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
Method
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, half the spices, salt for 30 min. Grill or broil until charred.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger in butter until golden.
  3. Add remaining spices and tomatoes; simmer 15 min then blend smooth.
  4. Stir in cream and butter; simmer 5 min.
  5. Add chicken and fenugreek; simmer 10 min. Serve with basmati rice or naan.

Salmon Teriyaki

⏱ 20 min 👥 2 servings Easy

Glossy lacquered salmon fillets glazed with a sweet-salty reduction of soy, mirin, and sake. Ready in 20 minutes.

Ingredients
  • 2 salmon fillets (skin-on, ~180g each)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake (or dry sherry)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Sesame seeds and scallions to garnish
Method
  1. Mix soy, mirin, sake, and sugar in a small bowl until sugar dissolves.
  2. Heat an oiled pan over medium-high. Cook salmon skin-side down 4 min.
  3. Flip and cook 2 min more. Pour sauce over and let it bubble and reduce.
  4. Spoon glaze repeatedly over fish for 1–2 min until lacquered. Serve over steamed rice.

Mexican

Tacos al Pastor

⏱ 1 hr 30 min 👥 6 servings Medium

Chile-marinated pork with caramelised pineapple, white onion, and fresh cilantro on warm corn tortillas.

Ingredients
  • 1kg pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 3 dried guajillo chiles, soaked
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo
  • 1/2 pineapple — half for marinade, half for serving
  • 4 garlic cloves, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp oregano
  • White onion, cilantro, corn tortillas to serve
Method
  1. Blend guajillo, chipotle, half the pineapple, garlic, cumin, oregano, and salt into a smooth marinade.
  2. Coat pork slices and marinate at least 1 hour (overnight preferred).
  3. Cook pork on a hot cast-iron pan in batches until charred and caramelised.
  4. Grill pineapple slices alongside. Chop everything coarsely.
  5. Serve in warm tortillas with onion, cilantro, and salsa.

French

French Onion Soup

⏱ 1 hr 15 min 👥 4 servings Medium

Deeply caramelised onions in a rich beef broth, crowned with crusty bread and a molten Gruyère gratin.

Ingredients
  • 1.2kg yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 150ml dry white wine or sherry
  • 1.2L good beef stock
  • 1 baguette, sliced; 160g Gruyère, grated
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme, salt and pepper
Method
  1. Melt butter and oil in a heavy pot. Add onions with a pinch of salt. Cook over low heat 45–55 min, stirring often, until deeply golden.
  2. Add wine and thyme; cook until evaporated.
  3. Pour in stock; simmer 15 min. Season.
  4. Toast baguette slices. Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls, top with bread and generous Gruyère.
  5. Broil 3–4 min until cheese is bubbling and golden. Serve immediately.

Shakshuka

⏱ 30 min 👥 3 servings Easy

Eggs poached in a spiced tomato and pepper sauce fragrant with cumin and smoked paprika. Brunch perfected.

Ingredients
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 × 400g cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, 4 garlic cloves
  • 1.5 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley, feta cheese, crusty bread to serve
Method
  1. Sauté onion and peppers in olive oil over medium heat until soft, ~8 min.
  2. Add garlic and all spices; cook 1 min until fragrant.
  3. Pour in tomatoes; season and simmer 10 min until slightly thickened.
  4. Make 6 wells in the sauce; crack an egg into each.
  5. Cover and cook 5–7 min until whites are set but yolks are still runny. Top with feta and parsley.

Thai

Pad Thai

⏱ 25 min 👥 2 servings Medium

Stir-fried rice noodles with shrimp, tofu, egg, and bean sprouts in a tangy tamarind and fish sauce glaze. A wok classic.

Ingredients
  • 200g flat rice noodles, soaked in warm water
  • 150g shrimp, peeled; 100g firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 eggs, 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3 tbsp tamarind paste, 2 tbsp fish sauce, 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 scallions, 3 tbsp roasted peanuts, 1 lime
  • Neutral oil for wok
Method
  1. Mix tamarind, fish sauce, and sugar into a sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat wok until smoking. Fry tofu until golden, push to side.
  3. Add shrimp and cook 1 min. Push aside, scramble eggs in centre.
  4. Add drained noodles and sauce; toss vigorously 2 min.
  5. Add bean sprouts and scallions; toss 30 sec. Serve topped with peanuts and lime.

French

Beef Bourguignon

⏱ 3 hrs 👥 6 servings Advanced

Tender braised beef in a deep burgundy wine sauce with pearl onions, lardons, and mushrooms. Julia would be pleased.

Ingredients
  • 1.2kg beef chuck, cut into 5cm pieces
  • 1 bottle burgundy or Pinot Noir
  • 200g lardons (thick-cut bacon)
  • 200g pearl onions, 300g cremini mushrooms
  • 2 carrots, 3 garlic cloves, 2 cups beef stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste, bouquet garni, 2 tbsp flour
Method
  1. Brown lardons in a Dutch oven; remove. In the fat, brown beef well on all sides. Remove.
  2. Sauté carrots and onions. Add garlic, tomato paste, flour.
  3. Return beef and lardons; pour in wine and stock. Add bouquet garni.
  4. Cover and braise in 160°C oven for 2.5 hours.
  5. Sauté pearl onions and mushrooms separately in butter; add to pot for final 15 min. Adjust seasoning.

American

Avocado Toast & Poached Egg

⏱ 15 min 👥 1 serving Easy

Whipped lemon ricotta meets smashed avocado on sourdough, topped with a trembling poached egg and chili flakes.

Ingredients
  • 2 thick slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp ricotta cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • Chili flakes, flaky salt, microgreens
Method
  1. Toast sourdough until golden and crispy.
  2. Whip ricotta with a fork; spread generously on toast.
  3. Mash avocado with lemon juice and flaky salt; layer over ricotta.
  4. Poach eggs in barely simmering water with vinegar for 3 min.
  5. Place egg on top; finish with chili flakes and microgreens.

Miso Ramen

⏱ 45 min 👥 2 servings Medium

A rich, warming bowl: umami-laden miso broth with ramen noodles, soft-boiled soy eggs, corn, and nori.

Ingredients
  • 2 portions fresh or dried ramen noodles
  • 3 tbsp white miso paste, 1 tbsp red miso paste
  • 1L chicken or dashi stock
  • 1 tbsp sesame paste (tahini), 1 tsp chili paste
  • 2 eggs (soft-boiled, soy-marinated)
  • Corn kernels, bamboo shoots, nori, sesame oil, scallions
Method
  1. Soft-boil eggs 6.5 min; cool, peel, and marinate in soy + mirin + water for at least 30 min.
  2. Whisk both misos, sesame paste, chili paste, and a ladle of hot stock until smooth.
  3. Bring remaining stock to a simmer. Stir in miso mixture. Do not boil.
  4. Cook noodles per package; drain and divide into bowls.
  5. Ladle broth over noodles; arrange halved eggs, corn, bamboo, and nori. Drizzle sesame oil.
