10 Tasty Recipes to Try

Explore a simple collection of easy, flavorful dishes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and dessert.

Breakfast

Banana Pancakes

Soft, fluffy pancakes with ripe banana sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Mash bananas and mix with eggs.
  2. Stir in flour and baking powder.
  3. Cook small rounds on a skillet until golden.
Lunch

Veggie Wraps

Crunchy wraps packed with fresh vegetables and hummus.

Ingredients

  • 4 tortillas
  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 carrot
  • Handful of spinach

Steps

  1. Spread hummus over each tortilla.
  2. Add sliced vegetables and spinach.
  3. Roll tightly and slice in half.
Dinner

Chicken Stir-Fry

A fast and colorful dinner with tender chicken and crisp vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken breast
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 broccoli head
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in a hot pan.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry until crisp-tender.
  3. Season with soy sauce and serve with rice.
Soup

Tomato Basil Soup

Warm and comforting with rich tomato flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 cup broth

Steps

  1. Saute onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes, broth, and basil.
  3. Simmer and blend until smooth.
Seafood

Baked Salmon

Simple baked salmon with lemon and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dill or parsley

Steps

  1. Season salmon with salt, pepper, and oil.
  2. Top with lemon slices and herbs.
  3. Bake at 200°C until flaky.
Snack

Chocolate Oat Cookies

Chewy cookies that are easy to make and share.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients until combined.
  2. Scoop onto a baking tray.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes.
Dinner

Beef Tacos

Loaded tacos with seasoned beef and fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • 300g ground beef
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 8 taco shells
  • Lettuce, salsa, cheese

Steps

  1. Brown beef with spices.
  2. Fill taco shells with beef.
  3. Top with lettuce, salsa, and cheese.
Dinner

Vegetable Fried Rice

Quick rice bowls with egg, peas, and carrots.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs in a wok.
  2. Add vegetables and rice.
  3. Season with soy sauce and stir until hot.
Dessert

Berry Yogurt Parfait

A fresh, layered dessert with fruit and crunchy granola.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Honey to drizzle

Steps

  1. Layer yogurt, berries, and granola in glasses.
  2. Repeat until full.
  3. Finish with a drizzle of honey.
Breakfast

Avocado Toast

Simple and satisfying toast with creamy avocado.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 avocado
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  3. Spread on toast and top with tomatoes.
